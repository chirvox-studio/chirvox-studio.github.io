FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to pay anything upfront?

Yes, we take 50% upfront to start the project and the remaining 50% when your website is ready to launch. This protects both parties.

What happens after my website is built?

We hand over everything to you — the files, access credentials, and a walkthrough. You can also opt for our monthly maintenance plan.

Can I update my website myself later?

Yes! We build simple static sites that you or anyone can update. We also offer a maintenance plan if you prefer us to handle it.

What is GitHub Pages hosting?

GitHub Pages is a free hosting service by GitHub. Your website is hosted for free, and it's fast and reliable. We handle the setup for you.

How do revisions work?

After the first draft, you give us feedback. Starter plan includes 1 revision round, Business plan includes 2. Additional revisions are available.

Will my website show up on Google?

The Business plan includes basic SEO setup that helps Google find your site. Local businesses typically start appearing in results within 1–4 weeks.
